About SHEcycle

Mission Statement

SHEcycle empowers college women to create a more sustainable, cost-effective wardrobe by connecting on-campus communities to swap, donate, and earn credit for gently used clothing. Our mission is to combat the overconsumption crisis and reduce the millions of clothing that end up in landfills each year. We believe in a sustainable future where style doesn’t come at the expense of the planet. College-aged women make up a high percentage of fast fashion consumers. By making college campuses our target audience, SHEcycle offers an eco-friendly alternative to fast fashion while supporting female empowerment and building a more sustainable future.

Swap ⋅ Style ⋅ Sustain

Our motto embodies our mission to transform the way we approach fashion in efforts to reduce the harmful waste to the environment.

Impact Statement

At SHEcycle, we’re actively working to reduce the waste generated by fast fashion—one piece of clothing at a time. Studies show that about 41% of young women feel pressured to not wear the same outfit twice, which makes fast fashion's cheap options particularly appealing. However, this trend comes at a heavy environmental cost. The average American consumer discards 81.5 pounds of clothing every year, much of which ends up in landfills, where it can take years to decompose. Worse, synthetic fabrics like polyester—commonly found in fast fashion—are not biodegradable.

The fashion industry is the second largest polluter globally, contributing to more carbon emissions than all international flights and maritime shipping combined. At SHEcycle, we’re committed to lessening the harmful impacts of fast fashion by providing sustainable alternatives. By highlighting the significant environmental toll of fast fashion, we aim to raise awareness about the importance of eco-conscious choices and demonstrate how SHEcycle can help reduce waste and promote a more sustainable approach to style.
