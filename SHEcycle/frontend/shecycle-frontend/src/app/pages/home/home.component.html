Welcome to SHEcycle

A sustainable fashion platform designed for college women to swap and donate gently used clothing. Join a movement that cuts down on waste, saves money, and empowers women—all while keeping fashion stylish and sustainable on your campus.

How It Works

📌 Sign Up

Create your free account and connect with students at your college to start swapping!

🛍️ Browse & List Items

Find items that match your style, or list your own gently used clothing to share with others.

🔄 Swap or Donate

Swap clothes with other users or donate items to earn credits for future purchases.

💰 Earn Credits

Earn credits for every item you donate and use them to get new clothes from the platform.

📦 Pick Up & Drop Off Locally

All exchanges happen on campus, reducing waste, shipping costs, and carbon emissions.

👗 Enjoy & Make an Impact

Wear clothes that save you money, reduce waste, and make fashion more sustainable—one swap at a time!

Swap ⋅ Style ⋅ Sustain

Join the movement towards a more sustainable future in fashion.
